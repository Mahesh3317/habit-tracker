# Quick Start Guide - Self Control & Habit Tracker 🨗

Get up and running with the app in 5 minutes!

## ⚡ Fast Setup (2 minutes)

### 1. Install Dependencies
```bash
cd "Habit change\habit-tracker"
npm install
```

### 2. Start the App
```bash
npm start
```

### 3. Open on Your Device
- Scan the QR code with your phone's camera
- The Expo Go app will open automatically
- Watch the app load and launch!

## 🎮 First Time Usage

### Dashboard Screen 📊
When you first open the app:
1. You'll see your starting stats (everything at 0)
2. Weekly progress shows 0% - this is normal!
3. Your streaks are empty - let's build them!

### Log Your First Day
1. Tap the **"Track"** tab at the bottom
2. Enter today's data:
   - **Cigarettes:** 0 (or your actual number)
   - **Avoid Masturbation:** Toggle on (✓)
   - **Workout:** Choose if you exercised
   - **Sleep:** Enter hours (e.g., 7.5)
3. You'll see completion indicators update

### Complete Daily Tasks
1. Tap the **"Tasks"** tab
2. Complete each task by clicking the checkbox:
   - ✓ Walk 20 minutes
   - ✓ Avoid porn
   - ✓ Reduce 1 cigarette
   - ✓ Eat breakfast
3. Earn 40+ points for your efforts!

### Handle an Urge (Critical!)
When you feel triggered:
1. Tap the **"Urge SOS"** tab (red alert icon)
2. Hit the big red button: "Start Emergency Timer"
3. The 5-minute countdown starts
4. Read the motivational messages
5. Try one of the suggested alternatives
6. You'll feel better!

## 📱 Key Features to Know

### Streaks 🔥
- **What:** Consecutive days of meeting your goals
- **Why:** They build momentum and willpower
- **How to maintain:** Log your data daily
- **Badges:** 3-day, 7-day, 30-day, and 100-day

### Points & Rewards 🎁
- Each task = 10 points
- Complete all tasks = 40 points (1 day)
- Save up points for motivation!
- No spending system yet - it's about the victory!

### Notifications 🔔
- **Morning (6 AM):** Get pumped up!
- **Evening (6 PM):** Stay strong against urges
- **Night (10 PM):** Sleep reminder
- Turn them on in Settings!

### Analytics 📈
- See your progress over 7 or 30 days
- Visual charts show your reduction
- Compare your performance vs goals

## 🔥 Pro Tips

### For Maximum Success
1. **Log immediately** after each habit (within an hour)
2. **Enable all notifications** - they help at critical moments
3. **Use Urge SOS immediately** when triggered (don't wait)
4. **Check streaks daily** - seeing the number grow is motivating
5. **Complete all tasks** - it only takes 30 minutes total

### Emergency Situations
- **Extreme urge?** Don't think, just tap Urge SOS
- **Close to breaking streak?** Open Analytics to see your progress
- **Feeling weak?** Read some motivational quotes in the app
- **Late logging?** Use Settings to check previous days

## 📞 Common Questions

**Q: Can I log data from previous days?**
A: Currently, the app logs for today. Future versions will allow backfill.

**Q: Will my data sync to the cloud?**
A: Not yet. It's stored locally on your phone. Don't uninstall without backup!

**Q: What if I need help with an urge?**
A: Tap Urge SOS immediately! It's designed exactly for this moment.

**Q: How long should I use this?**
A: At least 90 days to break the habit cycle. Some people use it forever for maintenance.

**Q: Can I reset and start over?**
A: Yes! In Settings, you can clear all data and start fresh.
