## 🔥 Core Screens Explained

### 1. **Dashboard** (Home)
Your main overview:
- Total points earned
- Weekly progress percentage
- All your streaks
- Badges collected

### 2. **Daily Tracker** (Track Tab)
Log your daily habits:
- Enter cigarettes (0-50)
- Toggle abstinence (yes/no)
- Log sleep hours (4-12)
- Check workout (yes/no)

### 3. **Urge SOS** (Red Alert)
When you feel triggered:
- Tap the big red button
- 5-minute timer starts
- Get motivation messages
- Try alternatives

### 4. **Tasks** (To-Do)
Daily objectives:
- Walk 20 minutes
- Avoid porn
- Reduce 1 cigarette
- Eat breakfast
- Earn points!

### 5. **Analytics** (Charts)
See your progress:
- 7-day or 30-day view
- Visual bar charts
- Reduction percentages
- Smart insights

### 6. **Settings** (Gear)
Control the app:
- Enable notifications
- View goals
- Clear app data
- Success tips

## 💪 Psychology Behind the App

### Why It Works:
1. **Tracking** - What gets measured gets managed
2. **Streaks** - Build identity through consistency
3. **Rewards** - Gamification keeps you engaged
4. **Support** - Urge SOS for crisis moments
5. **Progress** - See real improvement over time

### The Science:
- Urges last 5-15 minutes (survive them)
- Habits form in ~90 days
- Physical activity resets dopamine
- Consistency > Perfection

## ⚠️ Common Questions

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Local on your phone, nowhere else. Your privacy is protected.

**Q: Can I use this without internet?**
A: Yes! Everything works offline.

**Q: What if I miss a day?**
A: No problem! Restart immediately. Mistakes aren't failures.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Currently stores locally. Cloud sync coming soon.

**Q: Is this a replacement for therapy?**
A: No. It's a support tool. Seek professional help if needed.
